Our Services

What we do

Radio Type Approval

Radio Type Approval is the process to use the Radio and Telecommunications Terminal Equipment (“RTTE”) in the targeted country by getting an authorization (Certificate) from the Regulatory Telecom Authority.

Safety Compliance

Ensuring that your products meet the highest safety standards and regulatory requirements by following industry-specific safety protocols.

Shipment Compliance

Ensures every aspect of product logistics meets customs, safety, and telecom standards with proper documentation.

Accounts Registration Consultancy

A tailored service to assist businesses in smoothly registering their accounts with relevant authorities.

Regulations Consultancy

Expert support to help your business stay compliant with legal and industry-specific regulations.

Technical Translation

Specialized translations that are industry-specific, culturally aware, and technically precise for international success.
